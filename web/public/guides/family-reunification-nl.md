# Family Reunification → Netherlands Guide

## Overview
Join your family member who lives in the Netherlands.

## Who Can Sponsor
- Dutch citizens
- EU/EEA citizens with residence
- Non-EU with valid residence permit

## Who Can Apply
- Spouse/registered partner
- Children under 18
- Parents (in some cases)

## Requirements
**Sponsor must:**
- Have valid residence permit
- Earn at least 100% of minimum wage (€2,089.16/month gross in 2025)
- Have suitable housing
- Source: https://ind.nl/en/residence-permits/family-and-partner

**Applicant must:**
- Prove genuine relationship
- Pass civic integration exam abroad (some countries)
- Valid passport
- No criminal record
- Source: https://ind.nl/en/residence-permits/family-and-partner/partner

## Timeline
1. **Sponsor applies to IND** - Source: https://ind.nl/en/residence-permits/family-and-partner
2. **IND processes** (3-6 months)
3. **Applicant gets MVV** at consulate - Source: https://ind.nl/en/short-stay/mvv
4. **Travel to NL**
5. **Register at gemeente** - Source: https://www.government.nl/topics/personal-data/registering-with-your-municipality
6. **Collect residence permit**
7. **Complete integration course** within 3 years - Source: https://inburgeren.nl

## Costs
- Application fee: €210
- MVV: included
- Civic integration exam abroad: €150
