# Startup Visa → Netherlands Guide

## Overview
Entrepreneurs can get a startup visa to launch innovative businesses in NL.

## Requirements
- Innovative product/service
- Accepted by approved facilitator
- Sufficient funds (€1,500/month living costs)
- Valid passport
- Business plan

## Approved Facilitators
Find list at: https://ind.nl/en/residence-permits/work/start-up#facilitators

## Timeline
1. **Find facilitator** - Apply to startup incubator - Source: https://ind.nl/en/residence-permits/work/start-up#facilitators
2. **Get accepted** - Facilitator signs mentorship agreement
3. **Apply for residence permit** via facilitator - Source: https://ind.nl/en/residence-permits/work/start-up
4. **Receive MVV** at consulate (2-3 weeks)
5. **Travel to NL**
6. **Register at gemeente** - Source: https://www.government.nl/topics/personal-data/registering-with-your-municipality
7. **Build startup** (1 year permit, renewable)
8. **Switch to self-employed** after 1 year - Source: https://ind.nl/en/residence-permits/work/self-employed

## Costs
- Residence permit: €350
- Facilitator fees: varies (some free)
- Chamber of Commerce registration: €75

## After Startup Visa
Can switch to self-employed permit if business is viable.
