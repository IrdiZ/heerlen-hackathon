# Orientation Year (Zoekjaar) → Netherlands Guide

## Overview
1-year permit to find work after graduating from a top university.

## Eligibility
- Graduated within 3 years from:
  - Dutch university, OR
  - Top-200 world university (Times/QS/Shanghai ranking)
- Master's or PhD level
- Source: https://ind.nl/en/residence-permits/work/orientation-year-for-graduates

## Requirements
- Diploma from qualifying institution
- Valid passport
- Sufficient funds (€1,500/month)
- No previous orientation year

## Timeline
1. **Check university ranking** - Source: https://ind.nl/en/residence-permits/work/orientation-year-for-graduates
2. **Apply to IND** - Can apply from abroad or in NL - Source: https://ind.nl/en/residence-permits/work/orientation-year-for-graduates
3. **Receive permit** (2-4 weeks)
4. **Search for work** (1 year)
5. **Find job → switch to HSM** - Source: https://ind.nl/en/residence-permits/work/highly-skilled-migrant
6. **Or start business → switch to startup/self-employed**

## Benefits
- Can work any job during search
- No salary requirement during orientation year
- Easy switch to HSM once job found

## Costs
- Application fee: €210
- No income requirement during search
