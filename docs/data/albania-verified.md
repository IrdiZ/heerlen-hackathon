# Albania to Netherlands - Officially Verified Guide

> **Last verified:** 2026-02-07
> **Sources:** NetherlandsWorldwide.nl, IND.nl

---

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT: No Dutch Visa Services in Albania

**There is NO Dutch embassy or consulate in Albania that handles visa applications.**

Albanian citizens must apply at the **Dutch Embassy in Skopje, North Macedonia**.

**Source:** [NetherlandsWorldwide - MVV Albania](https://www.netherlandsworldwide.nl/visa-the-netherlands/mvv-long-stay/apply-albania)

---

## MVV Application Process for Albanian Citizens

### Where to Apply
After IND approval, collect your MVV visa sticker at:

**Dutch Embassy in Skopje, North Macedonia**

Your IND approval letter will specify this location.

**Source:** [NetherlandsWorldwide - MVV North Macedonia](https://www.netherlandsworldwide.nl/visa-the-netherlands/mvv-long-stay/apply-north-macedonia)

---

## Making an Appointment

### Online Booking:
[Book appointment at Embassy Skopje via VFS Global](https://www.netherlandsworldwide.nl/making-appointment/north-macedonia)

### Important Notes:
- Book **within 3 months** of IND approval letter
- Plan travel to Skopje in advance
- Confirmation sent by email

---

## Required Documents

Bring to your appointment:
- Valid passport
- IND approval letter
- Passport photos (biometric)
- Proof of payment (if applicable)

*Exact requirements depend on visa type - check your IND letter*

---

## MVV Sticker Collection

- Processing time varies
- Embassy will email when passport with MVV is ready
- **No appointment needed** for collection
- **Verify all information** on sticker immediately

---

## Travel to Skopje

From Tirana to Skopje:
- **By bus:** ~4-5 hours
- **By car:** ~3-4 hours (via Struga border crossing)

Plan accommodation if needed - you may need to return for collection.

---

## Document Legalization

Albanian documents may need:
- **Apostille** (Albania is Hague Convention member since 2004)
- Translation by sworn translator

*Check specific requirements with IND*
