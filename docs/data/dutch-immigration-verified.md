## Highly Skilled Migrant (Kennismigrant) Visa

### Salary Requirements 2026

| Category | Gross Monthly Salary (excl. holiday allowance) |
|----------|----------------------------------------------|
| Age 30+ | €5,942 |
| Under 30 | €4,357 |
| Reduced criterion* | €3,122 |
| European Blue Card | €5,942 |
| Blue Card reduced* | €4,754 |

*Reduced criterion applies if you graduated from Dutch higher education within 3 years.

**Source:** [IND Required Amounts 2026](https://ind.nl/en/required-amounts-income-requirements)

### Key Requirements

1. **Recognized Sponsor**: Your employer MUST be IND-recognized
   - Check: [IND Public Register of Recognized Sponsors](https://ind.nl/en/public-register-recognised-sponsors)

2. **Employment Contract**: Must meet salary threshold for your age bracket

3. **Valid Passport**: At least 6 months validity recommended

4. **MVV (Entry Visa)**: Required for most non-EU nationalities
   - Employer applies to IND
   - After approval, you collect MVV sticker at embassy

**Source:** [IND Highly Skilled Migrant](https://ind.nl/en/residence-permits/work/highly-skilled-migrant)

## After Arrival in Netherlands

### 1. Municipality Registration (Gemeente)
- Register within 5 days of arrival
- Bring: passport, birth certificate, rental contract
- You'll receive your BSN (citizen service number)

### 2. BSN (Burgerservicenummer)
- Issued at gemeente registration
- Required for: bank account, health insurance, employment

### 3. Health Insurance (Zorgverzekering)
- **MANDATORY** within 4 months of arrival
- Basic package ~€130-150/month
- Apply for zorgtoeslag (healthcare allowance) if eligible

### 4. DigiD
- Apply online after receiving BSN
- Takes ~1 week to receive activation code by post
- Required for: taxes, healthcare allowance, government services

### 5. 30% Tax Ruling
- Employer must apply within 4 months of start date
- Reduces taxable income by 30%
- Must meet: salary threshold, recruited from abroad, specific expertise
