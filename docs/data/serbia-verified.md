## MVV Application Process for Serbian Citizens

### Where to Apply
After IND approval, collect your MVV visa sticker at:

**Dutch Embassy in Belgrade**
- Address: **Milentija Popovica 5b, 11070 Belgrade, Serbia**

Your IND approval letter will confirm this location.

**Source:** [NetherlandsWorldwide - MVV Serbia](https://www.netherlandsworldwide.nl/visa-the-netherlands/mvv-long-stay/apply-serbia)

## Making an Appointment

### Online Booking:
[Book appointment via VFS Global](https://www.vfsvisaonline.com/Netherlands-Global-Online-Appointment_Zone2/AppScheduling/AppWelcome.aspx?P=KWfLdX%2FGTmuYQUeqIQEOCNnhmBT2ceX%2Bc%2FqmPvZwnLg%3D)

### Important Notes:
- Book **within 3 months** of IND approval letter
- Confirmation sent by email
- **Appointments fill up quickly** due to high demand - book early!

**Source:** [NetherlandsWorldwide - Appointments Serbia](https://www.netherlandsworldwide.nl/making-appointment/serbia)

## Required Documents

Bring to your appointment:
- Valid passport
- IND approval letter
- Passport photos (biometric)
- Proof of payment (if applicable)

*Exact requirements depend on visa type - check your IND letter*

---

## MVV Sticker Collection

- Processing time: **Up to 10 working days**
- You'll be told at appointment when to collect
- **Verify all information** on sticker immediately at the desk

---

## Document Legalization

Serbian documents may need:
- **Apostille** from Serbian court (for Hague Convention documents)
- Translation by sworn translator

*Check specific requirements with IND*
