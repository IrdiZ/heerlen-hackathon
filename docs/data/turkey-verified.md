# Turkey to Netherlands - Officially Verified Guide

> **Last verified:** 2026-02-07
> **Sources:** NetherlandsWorldwide.nl, IND.nl

---

## MVV Application Process for Turkish Citizens

### Where to Apply
After IND approval, collect your MVV visa sticker at:
- **Dutch Embassy in Ankara**, OR
- **Dutch Consulate-General in Istanbul**

Your IND approval letter specifies which location.

**Source:** [NetherlandsWorldwide - MVV Turkey](https://www.netherlandsworldwide.nl/visa-the-netherlands/mvv-long-stay/apply-turkiye)

---

## Making an Appointment

### For Highly Skilled Migrants / Work Permits:
1. Email your flight details to: **ank-atv@minbuza.nl**
2. VFS Global will call you to schedule appointment

### Important Notes:
- Make appointment **within 3 months** of IND approval letter
- Family reunification as refugee: 6 months deadline
- Appointments fill up quickly - apply early

**Source:** [NetherlandsWorldwide - Appointments Turkey](https://www.netherlandsworldwide.nl/making-appointment/turkiye)

---

## Required Documents

Bring to your appointment:
- Valid passport
- IND approval letter
- Passport photos (biometric)
- Proof of payment (if applicable)

*Exact requirements depend on visa type - check your IND letter*

---

## MVV Sticker Collection

- Processing time: **Up to 10 working days**
- Options: Collect in person OR have it sent to you
- **Verify all information** on sticker immediately (name, validity dates)

---

## Embassy Contact

**Netherlands Embassy Ankara**
- VFS Global handles appointments
- Email: ank-atv@minbuza.nl

**Netherlands Consulate-General Istanbul**
- For applications in Istanbul region

**General Contact:** [NetherlandsWorldwide Contact](https://www.netherlandsworldwide.nl/contact/embassies-consulates-general/turkiye)

## EU-Turkey Association Agreement

Turkish citizens may have additional rights under the EU-Turkey Association Agreement (Ankara Agreement). This can affect:
- Work permit requirements
- Family reunification rules
- Fee reductions in some cases

*Consult IND or immigration lawyer for specific eligibility*
